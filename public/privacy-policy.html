Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 19, 2026

Privacy First: Job Hunt Dashboard is a personal productivity tool. Your data stays with you. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Gmail Data

When you grant access to your Gmail account, the Application accesses:

  • Email metadata: Sender, subject, date, email ID
  • Email content: Body text of job-related emails only
  • Attachments: None (we do not access attachments)

Scope of Access: The Application only searches for and reads emails related to job searching (e.g., emails from LinkedIn, Indeed, recruiters, company career pages). We do not access personal correspondence, financial emails, or other non-job-related messages.

1.2 Job Application Data

Information you provide or we extract from job listings:

  • Job titles, company names, locations
  • Job URLs and descriptions
  • Salary information (when available)
  • Application status and dates
  • Notes you add about jobs or interviews

1.3 Usage Data

We do not collect analytics or usage tracking data. No cookies, no tracking pixels, no third-party analytics.

2. How We Use Your Information

Purpose Data Used
Display job opportunities in your dashboard Extracted job information from emails and APIs
Track your application progress Application status, dates, notes you provide
Prevent duplicate job listings Job URLs and company/title combinations
Research companies (if enabled) Company names for web searches
Filter and score job relevance Job descriptions, titles, locations, salaries

3. Data Storage and Security

3.1 Where Your Data is Stored

  • Self-hosted PostgreSQL: Job listings, application tracking data, and notes on your own server
  • Your Browser: OAuth tokens for Gmail access (stored in browser local storage)
  • Not Stored: Full email content, email attachments, personal emails

3.2 Data Retention

  • Job data: Retained until you manually delete it
  • Gmail access token: Stored in browser until you revoke access or clear browser data
  • Email content: Never stored; only processed temporarily to extract job information

3.3 Security Measures

  • All data transmission uses HTTPS encryption
  • Gmail access uses OAuth 2.0 (secure authorization standard)
  • Database access is restricted to your server-side API and local admin access
  • No passwords stored (authentication via OAuth)
  • Application hosted on secure infrastructure (Netlify)

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

4.1 We Do Not Share Your Data

Your personal information and Gmail data are never sold, rented, or shared with third parties for marketing, advertising, or any other commercial purposes.

4.2 Third-Party Services

The Application uses these third-party services:

  • Google Gmail API: To access your job-related emails. See Google's Privacy Policy
  • Netlify: For application hosting. See Netlify Privacy Policy
  • Job Board APIs/Sources: Adzuna and publicly available job listing pages used for intake

4.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law, such as to comply with a subpoena or similar legal process, or when we believe in good faith that disclosure is necessary to protect our rights, your safety, or the safety of others.

5. Your Rights and Choices

5.1 Access and Control

  • View your data: All job data is visible in your dashboard
  • Edit data: Update job information, application status, notes at any time
  • Delete data: Remove individual jobs or clear your entire database
  • Export data: Contact us for a data export (JSON format)

5.2 Revoke Gmail Access

You can revoke the Application's access to your Gmail at any time:

  1. Visit Google Account Permissions
  2. Find "Job Hunt Dashboard" in the list
  3. Click "Remove Access"

5.3 Delete Your Account

To permanently delete all your data, contact us at hello@mattlelonek.co.uk. We will delete all your job data from our database within 30 days.

6. Gmail API Limited Use Disclosure

Job Hunt Dashboard's use and transfer of information received from Google APIs adheres to Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

Specifically:

  • Gmail data is only used to provide job search aggregation functionality
  • We do not use Gmail data for serving advertisements
  • We do not allow humans to read your Gmail data (automated processing only)
  • We do not transfer Gmail data to third parties (except as necessary to provide the service, comply with applicable law, or as part of a merger/acquisition with notice)
